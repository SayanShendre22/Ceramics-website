GAJANAND CERAMICS An ISO 9001 : 2015 Certified


Welcome to Gajanand Ceramics, where we’ve spent the last 15 years perfecting the art of manufacturing to deliver unparalleled quality and innovation. Our journey has been one of continuous growth, learning, and evolution, and today, we proudly stand as a leader in crafting top-notch paper tubes, tundish boards, ramming mass, and temperature tips.


Paper Tubes: Our paper tubes are more than cylindrical containers; they’re a result of precision engineering and craftsmanship. Whether it’s for packaging, storage, or any other application, our paper tubes are designed to meet the highest standards, ensuring durability, strength, and eco-friendliness.


Tundis Boards: Discover excellence in insulation with our tundis boards. These carefully engineered boards are built to provide exceptional thermal and sound insulation. Whether you’re working on construction projects or industrial applications, our tundis boards deliver performance that’s as reliable as it is innovative.


Ramming Mass: In the world of metallurgy, our ramming mass is your trusted companion. Specially formulated to withstand high temperatures and harsh conditions, our ramming mass ensures optimal furnace lining, contributing to efficient metal production and casting processes.


Temperature Tips: Precision and accuracy are paramount when it comes to temperature measurement. Our temperature tips combine cutting-edge technology with years of expertise to provide you with accurate readings even in challenging environments. Rely on our temperature tips for consistent and reliable results.


Since its establishment in 2009, our company has seen great success due to its consistent determination to achieve high standards Products in quality, customer service, and manufacturing of quality products, primarily aimed at providing quality products, and hence our motto.
